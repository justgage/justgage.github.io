Roam's Model Sucks for Mobile

  • Bullet Points aren't really mobile friendly
    • You can't drag them very easily
    • There's no "Tab" key
  • Wiki Links aren't easy to type
    • [[ is really hard to get to on a mobile keyboard. Most people probably don't even know how to
  • References V.S. Embeds are too complex and fiddly
    • What most people want to do is just collect a bunch of ideas and eventually turn them into something new.
  • Moving around text is really hard because text selection is hard.
  • Doing secondary actions on a paragraph doesn't work because it would cause the text to select. You could click on the bullet, but this would be likewise difficult and counter intuitive and tricky.
  • Trying to insert your cursor between two paragraphs is hard(ish)
  • Roam is all about combing information and doing some serious transformations on them, mobile doesn't offer much screen real estate to see much information at once.
  • Searching by typing is awkward, although perhaps still the most accurate.
  • Roam is a web app so it's limited by the browser.
