Don't censor yourself - Writing down the bones

obsessions p38

One of my obsessions is writing about my Jewish family... But when I make a conscious decision to to not write about my family, the act of repressing it seems to repress everything else to, simply because I am spending a lot of energy avoiding something.

This is how I feel about Slack, I write about it constantly. I'm obsessed. It's terrible and probably unhealthy but repressing it made everything else shut down. I can't censor myself, it's just unhealthy because it makes everything else in my life shut down.
