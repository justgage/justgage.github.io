Pitches

Shape Up helps a lot because it creates a central document (called a Pitch) that's detailed enough to accomplish the goal in a reasonable amount of time, but not too detailed to allow flexibility in scope. Also setting time constraints allows everyone in the company to make better tradeoffs. Before anything new is added it's more common to as, "But is this going to make us miss our deadline?"
