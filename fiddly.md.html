fiddly

when a particular UI interaction requires lots of clicks and thought to interact with it.

For instance in HEY! they use a simple stacking method for "later" stuff

Which is less fiddly than the typical "snooze" feature most email clients have started to implement:

initial interaction

date picker

They Hey thing only has very simple interactions:

  1. I don't want to forget about this, so I'll "set it aside" (place it in the stack)
  2. I'm done with this email, so I'll remove it from the stack

Whereas the "snooze" feature presents you with way too many choices and gets in your face much more than necessary

  1. Do you want to process it today, tomorrow, someday (never), next week, or any time in the world?
  2. ...Email shows up
  3. Dang, can't look at this right now, I'll snooze it again... (loop)
