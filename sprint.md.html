sprint

a thing in Agile where you work on a mini project. No longer than one month and most commonly two weeks. The main point of these Sprints is to avoid projects from taking years and to make Estimation easier.

The downside is they often turn into Neverending Sprints where you just have a project that never ends and has no clear goal. Small improvements are made but it's hard to see much progress. You, end up wandering more than anything. People loose Motivation and generally just get lazy.
