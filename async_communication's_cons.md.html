Async Communication's Cons

Asynchronous Communication has the following problems:

  • Not as human, people often don't feel as attached to the people on the other end. Can encourage the problems in Reclaming Conversation.
  • Not as engaging, people often ignore it if they can, even by accident. While this is more Humane Tech, it also can be too easy to ignore.

Other than that I still love it.
