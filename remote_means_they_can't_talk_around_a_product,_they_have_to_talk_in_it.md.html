remote means they can't talk around a product, they have to talk in it

When people do Stand-up they often stand around a JIRA board or a Whiteboard. The communication happens around the product. People can point to a card with their finger or make comments anywhere on the page. You don't need to talk in context because you can just do that with normal human facilities.
