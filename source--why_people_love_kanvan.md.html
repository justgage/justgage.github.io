Here are the key Kanban benefits:

  1. visually organizes all workflow segments
  2. defines bottlenecks quickly in real-time
  3. motivates teams to collaborate to optimize the whole value chain rather than just their part
  4. succeed in cases when operations and support teams have a high rate of uncertainty and variability
  5. looks good for spreading throughout the company naturally
  6. reduces inventory and decrease the costs
  7. excludes overproduction of inventory
