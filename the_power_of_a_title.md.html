The power of a title

Titles are super powerful, they allow you to summarize a huge amount of data that they contain.

Good Examples

Basecamp's message board is a good example as well as Twist, email, and most Asynchronous Communication tools.

Bad Examples

Bad examples are Slack which has no titles and force the user to read everything.
