Writing (or Coding) lots is not enough

This advice is roughly similar for becoming better at writing code. Volume is a prerequisite, but it’s also not sufficient. Critically examining those words - prose or code - after they’re written is part of the progress. You can write the same crap a million times. ~ DHH
