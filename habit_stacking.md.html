Habit Stacking

The idea that you keep chaining habits onto each other, one after another. For instance you keep adding small habits to a morning routine such as:

  • Read Scriptures
  • Read Informative book
  • Write in Journal
  • Plan out Day

Usually this requires a Keystone Habit to start the chain.
