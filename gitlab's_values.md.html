Gitlab's Values

Gitlab has a ton of great values that can be found on this page.

GitLab's six values are

  • 🤝 Collaboration
  • 📈 Results
  • ⏱️ Efficiency
  • 🌐 Diversity, Inclusion & Belonging
  • 👣 Iteration
  • 👁️ Transparency

Together they spell the CREDIT we give each other by assuming good intent.
