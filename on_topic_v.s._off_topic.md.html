on topic V.S. off topic

An example of a comment that's "on topic" would be:

"A hotdog isn't a sandwitch but it is a Taco"

But not:

"I made a hotdog today"

Or

"My dog is so hot, it's like 90 degrees outside"

A good example of things that aren't on topic... or even questions is this Youtube video: Studio C - No Stupid Questions
