Keep Conversation a game of Ping-Pong

Conversations need to have flow. If people send you a message, try to have a good response but perhaps more important is to not to forget to respond so that they don't think you aren't listening.

It's important in Asynchronous Communication to have full context but it's also important to actually respond. It's easy to forget, especially with text messages and emails.
